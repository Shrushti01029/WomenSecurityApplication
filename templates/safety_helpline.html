⚠ What To Do In Emergency

  • Stay calm and assess your surroundings.
  • Move to a safe and populated area.
  • Call the appropriate emergency helpline immediately.
  • Share your live location with trusted contacts.
  • Collect evidence (photo/video) if safe.
  • Report through Incident Reporting System.

🥋 Self-Defense Awareness

Learning basic self-defense techniques improves confidence, awareness, and the ability to react quickly in threatening situations. Consider enrolling in certified self-defense or martial arts training programs.
